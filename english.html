PRINT PRICE

By deselecting the Print Price button, the prices of all the lines in the group are hidden. It is not possible to hide the price of a single line (it can be done manually from Word or by not inserting the line on the CPQ).
Examples:

  • if the margin of the robots is modified from the standard values (30% Export), automatically Print Price is deselected
  • if the margin of non-robot products is changed from the standard (15% Export), Print Price will remain selected (the "pumped" price will appear directly in print)
  • if hours are added on the robot, the Print Price must be deselected manually
  • if the margin of the robots is higher than 50%, it will be signaled by a message and must be corrected
  • if the discount is greater than the margin, it will be signaled by a message and must be corrected
  • for services the price is always visible, you can set the price as 0 and top up the price elsewhere
